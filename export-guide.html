How to Export Your Game Data

Step-by-step guides for each supported game

⚓ World of Warships: Legends

WoWS Legends lets you request a full export of your account data through Wargaming's GDPR data portal. The export includes your battle stats, ship records, session history, and more.

Step 1: Go to the Wargaming Data Export Page

Visit the Wargaming personal data page for your platform:

  • PlayStation / Xbox / PC: https://wargaming.net/personal_data/

Log in with the same Wargaming account linked to your console.

Step 2: Request Your Data

  1. Once logged in, click "Request Data Export" (or similar button)
  2. Select World of Warships: Legends from the game list if prompted
  3. Confirm your request

Wargaming will prepare your data export. This can take anywhere from a few minutes to 24 hours.

Step 3: Download Your Export

  1. You'll receive an email notification when your export is ready
  2. Return to the personal data page
  3. Download the .zip file

Step 4: Upload to GamingDiver

  1. Go to GamingDiver
  2. Drag and drop the .zip file onto the upload area (or click to browse)
  3. Your data is processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server

What's in the Export?

The zip file contains several CSV files with your data:

  • Ship Statistics — per-ship battle counts, XP, distance traveled
  • Ship Statistics by Type — per-ship stats broken down by game mode (Standard, Ranked, etc.)
  • Battle Type Statistics — overall stats per game mode
  • Game Sessions — start/end times of every play session
  • Account Statistics — account-level summary
  • Account Storage — items, commanders, and other account data

Tips

  • You can request a new export any time to get updated stats
  • The export includes all your historical data, not just recent matches
  • GamingDiver caches your results locally, so you don't need to re-upload every visit
